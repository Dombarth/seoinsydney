Everything you need to know about MacBook battery replacement

Most MacBook battery replacements are completed within 1-2 hours. You can drop off your MacBook and pick it up the same day. We also offer express service for urgent repairs.

Every battery replacement comes with a comprehensive 12-month warranty. If you experience any issues with the battery during this period, we'll replace it free of charge.

We use premium quality batteries that meet or exceed original specifications. While we don't use Apple-branded batteries (which are not available for purchase), our batteries are sourced from reputable manufacturers and provide equivalent or better performance.

If your MacBook is still under Apple's warranty, we recommend checking with Apple first. For out-of-warranty MacBooks, our professional replacement service will not cause any issues. We use proper tools and techniques to ensure no damage to your device.

Signs your battery needs replacement include: battery draining quickly, MacBook shutting down unexpectedly, "Service Battery" warning in System Preferences, battery not charging to 100%, or the battery is swollen. We offer free diagnostics to confirm.

Yes! We offer mobile repair service across Sydney for an additional fee. Our technician can come to your home or office and replace the battery on-site. Contact us at 0400 454 859 to arrange mobile service.

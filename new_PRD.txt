Below is the **complete final Product Requirements Document (PRD)** for the next full build phase of **seoinsydney.com**, incorporating the existing sitemap, identified gaps, conversion funnel architecture, keyword targeting, and long-term scalability. This document is intended to be implementation-ready for design, development, SEO, and content teams.

---

# Product Requirements Document

## Project

SEOInSydney.com Lead Acquisition Platform Expansion

## Objective

Transform the website from a location-based SEO presence into a full local acquisition system that:

1. Captures high-intent “SEO services Sydney” searches
2. Captures beginner users who do not know SEO terminology
3. Converts suburb traffic into service enquiries
4. Establishes authority in both commercial and informational search clusters
5. Creates scalable internal linking architecture to dominate Sydney SEO search results

Primary KPI targets (12 months):

* 3× increase in organic enquiries
* Top 3 rankings for “SEO Sydney” core cluster
* 2× increase in non-brand organic traffic
* 5%+ sitewide enquiry conversion rate
* Ranking presence across suburb + industry + beginner keyword clusters

## 2.2 Publishing cadence

First 90 days:

* Launch SEO services pillar
* Launch beginner hub (5 pages)
* Launch 5 industry pages
* Publish 12 supporting blog articles
* Publish 3 case studies

Ongoing:

* 4 blog articles monthly
* 1 case study monthly
* 1 new industry page quarterly

---

# 3. Conversion System Requirements

Every page must include:

Primary CTA:

* Free SEO Audit

Secondary CTA:

* Request SEO Strategy

Placement:

* Hero section
* Mid-content block
* End-of-page CTA
* Sticky header CTA
* Footer enquiry form

Lead forms must:

* Submit to CRM automatically
* Trigger automated response email
* Allow quick submission (name, email, website)

---

# 4. Internal Linking Architecture

Rules:

* Suburb pages link upward to service pillar
* Blog pages link to both beginner hub and service pages
* Industry pages link to both service pages and suburb pages
* Beginner pages link to service conversion pages
* Service pages link downward to suburbs and industries

Goal: create a hierarchical authority funnel directing ranking signals to commercial pages.

# 9. Strategic Outcome

After full deployment the website will function as:

* A high-intent commercial SEO service funnel
* A beginner acquisition channel capturing users before competitors
* A suburb-level local ranking network feeding authority upward
* A scalable authority content ecosystem
* A continuously compounding organic lead generation system

This structure positions the domain to dominate both **“SEO Sydney” commercial searches** and **plain-language beginner searches**, ensuring long-term compounding growth in rankings, authority, and lead volume.
